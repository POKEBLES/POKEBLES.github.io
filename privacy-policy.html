1. Introduction

Manforest Graphics and Design Services, Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

By using our website and services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not access our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Request a quotation
  • Place an order
  • Contact us via email, phone, or Viber
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Fill out forms on our website

This may include:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Company or business name
  • Project details and specifications
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Communication preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website addresses
  • Clickstream data

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. See our Cookies Policy section for more details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Prepare quotations and invoices
  • Communicate about your projects
  • Schedule deliveries and installations
  • Provide customer support

3.2 Account Management

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Authenticate user access
  • Send order confirmations and updates

3.3 Communication

  • Respond to inquiries and requests
  • Send administrative information
  • Provide customer service
  • Send newsletters and promotional materials (with your consent)

3.4 Business Operations

  • Improve our website and services
  • Analyze usage trends and preferences
  • Conduct market research
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • Payment processors
  • Shipping and delivery partners
  • Email service providers
  • Web hosting services
  • Analytics providers

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities
  • Protect the safety of users and the public

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Our security measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee training on data protection

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), you have the following rights:

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to access, correct, or update your personal information.

6.2 Data Portability

You may request a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used format.

6.3 Erasure

You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.

6.4 Objection

You may object to the processing of your personal information for certain purposes.

6.5 Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw your consent for marketing communications at any time by:

  • Clicking "unsubscribe" in our emails
  • Contacting us directly
  • Updating your account preferences

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section.

7. Cookies Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website.

7.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your browsing to show relevant ads

7.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

For significant changes, we will provide prominent notice or seek your consent as required by law.
